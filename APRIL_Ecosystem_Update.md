# April 2026 Ecosystem Update: Ultimate Integration

The April 2026 Talent Stacks Event focuses on bridging the gap between Bitcoin-native assets and Clarity smart contracts. This update introduces a series of new contracts and tools designed to enhance builder reputation and track ecosystem impact.

### Phase 2: Impact & Analytics (April 11 - April 20)

### Phase 3: Governance & Reputation (April 21 - April 30)

- **StacksRank SDK**: Standardized way to fetch builder stats.
